Markov chains

Named after mathematician Andrey Markov, The aim of using Markov chains is to provide probabilities for the occurrence of future events. Markov chains are used in many fields, such as Physics, Chemistry, Games and many more!

For more information visit https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markov_chain
